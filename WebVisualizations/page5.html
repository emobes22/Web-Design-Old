City Latitude vs. Wind Speed

The final scatter plot reveals the relationship between latitude and wind speed. Again, we’re not seeing a strong positive or negative correlation, but we do see some patterns. Wind speeds between 0 and 5 miles per hour (mph) seem to be the most common. Additionally, we’re seeing larger variances in wind speeds, as you move further away from the equator, at least on this particular day. In the equator region, the windspeed ranged from 1mph to 14mph, while at 60 degrees latitude the windspeed ranged from 2mph to 30mph.
