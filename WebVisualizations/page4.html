City Latitude vs. Cloudiness

In this graph, city latitudes and their respective cloudiness percentage were measured. Here we do not see a strong positive or negative relationship between the two variables. As latitude increases, the amount of clouds in the sky do not appear to increase or decrease. What you do see is a 0% cloudiness metric being very commonly recorded, so it looks pretty common to have no clouds in the sky.
